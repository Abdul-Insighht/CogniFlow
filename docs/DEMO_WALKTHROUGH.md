# CogniFlow Demo Walkthrough

## Goal

Show that CogniFlow turns student overload into a clear, human-approved action plan.

## Steps For Judges

1. Open `index.html`.
2. Click `Load sample`.
3. Observe the cognitive state badge, stress meter, confidence score, and detected signals.
4. Read the recommended plan.
5. Click `Edit`, change one line, and save it to show human control.
6. Go to `Memory`.
7. Search: `What should I revise for DBMS?`
8. Show that CogniFlow retrieves the saved DBMS note.
9. Go to `AI Guardrails`.
10. Explain privacy, transparency, and non-medical boundaries.

## What The Demo Proves

- CogniFlow accepts realistic student workload input.
- It classifies cognitive load from natural language and task pressure.
- It retrieves relevant knowledge from the student's stored notes.
- It adapts the task plan based on the student's state.
- It keeps the student in control through accept, edit, and reject actions.
